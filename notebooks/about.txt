This folder consists of the script used to aggregate news using RSS 

File 1: rss-ds-blog-aggregator-streamlit.py- An expandable dictionary of datascience blog rss links, which are aggregated and displayed at your localhost address using streamlit
File 2: rss-news-aggregator-terminal.py- An expandable dictionary of popular news agency rss links which are aggregated and displayed at your local terminal.
